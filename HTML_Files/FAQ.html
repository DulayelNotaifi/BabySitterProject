FAQs

Parents FAQs:

Q/ what differentiates A Watchful Eye?

A/ With A Watchful Eye you'll have the confidence and calm that comes with having the ideal babysitter for your children. So you can manage the busy juggling act of children, school, work, social and family life. You get freedom, choice and flexibility. Freedom to connect with babysitters directly. Choice to select the best babysitter for your family from a wide range of trusted babysitters. Flexibility to arrange the hours and days that you need. So whether it's time to return to work, time to get help with your family, or time to get a moment for yourself, A Watchful Eye can always help you!

Q/ Is it free to register?

A/ Yes! It is free to register.

Q/ Do your babysitters have other educational or cultural skills?

A/ Our babysitters have listed a range of wonderful skills and interests on their profiles. Many speak languages (Like: English, French, German, Spanish, etc), play music (guitar and piano), play sport, dance or create art. So you can choose a babysitter to bring specific skills to your children and family!

Q/ How much should I pay my babysitter?

A/ You are free to negotiate rates with your babysitter, keeping the market-demand and industry rates in mind.

Q/ How do I leave feedback about sitters?

A/ Parents can post a rating and write a review about babysitters they have booked on the "Rating and Review " page or they can send their feedback to us via "contact us" page. We welcome your feedback as it helps us and other parents to assess the babysitter at A Watchful Eye, and it also helps the babysitter to improve.

Babysitters FAQs:

Q/ What is A Watchful Eye?

A/ It is a website for babysitters and parents. An easy way for you to meet parents and find jobs that suit you.

Q/ Is it free to register?

A/ Yes! It is free to register.

Q/ What requirements are there for this type of work?

A/ Most parents are looking for sitters who have experience, are trustworthy, loving, caring and responsible.

Q/ How do I set my hourly rate?

A/ You set your own rates, based on your qualifications, age, skills and experience. Rates may also vary according to the duties required and the location of the job.

Q/ How do the 'parent ratings' work? How can I get positive ratings?

A/ Parents may write a brief comment about your work and give you a rating. Great ratings are achieved by carers who are responsive, reliable and excellent babysitters!
